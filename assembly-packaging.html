Sterile Sealing

Pouch Sealing & Ribbon Packing

Vidhata Plastics provides cleanroom-validated heat sealing solutions for medical devices. Our pouch packing setups process standard poly pouches and breathable Tyvek® pouches, ensuring secure seal joints and easy peelable access for clinical end-users.

We also offer high-efficiency **ribbon packing** configurations for medical disposables, ensuring high seal reliability and complete protection against microbial ingress. Every seal is monitored for dwell time, temperature, and cooling pressure.

Blister Sealing

Blister Packaging & Rigid Trays

For delicate or heavy medical devices, we provide protective **blister packing** and **rigid thermoformed trays**. These trays keep the device stationary inside the package, protecting the catheter tips, connectors, or surgical devices from physical stress.

Thermoformed trays are heat-sealed with porous Tyvek® lidding material to allow complete gas entry during **ETO sterilization** cycles and rapid gas extraction during subsequent aeration phases.
