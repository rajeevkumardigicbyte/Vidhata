Who We Are

India's Premier Integrated Plastics Manufacturer

Vidhata Plastics India Pvt. Ltd. is a fully integrated plastics manufacturing company specializing in product design, tooling, mould manufacturing, injection moulding, extrusion, polyurethane processing, assembly, and cleanroom medical manufacturing.

With over 20 years of industry experience, Vidhata delivers end-to-end manufacturing solutions under one roof, helping customers reduce lead times, improve quality, and simplify supply chain management.

Our Journey

Two Decades of Growth

2003
Company Founded
Started as a precision toolroom in Hyderabad with a focus on quality mould manufacturing.
2007
ISO 9001 Certification
Achieved ISO 9001 quality certification and expanded into high-volume injection moulding.
2012
Medical Division Launch
Established dedicated cleanroom manufacturing facility for medical and pharmaceutical clients.
2016
ISO 13485 Certification
Obtained ISO 13485 medical device quality management certification. Onboarded NephroPlus and Cathronic Medical.
2019
Reliance Jio Partnership
Delivered 12 million charger housings for Reliance Jio — largest single order milestone.
2024
200M+ Components Milestone
Surpassed 200 million components produced and 5,000 MT annual raw material processing capacity.
