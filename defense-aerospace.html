High-Reliability Avionics & Defense Components

Vidhata Plastics engineering team co-develops high-performance plastic assemblies for critical defense systems and commercial aerospace applications. Utilizing high-performance polymers, our injection-molded components deliver extreme thermal stability, chemical inertness, and structural integrity.

We specialize in cockpit flight bezels, custom military connector housings, radomes, and electronic enclosure shells. Every component is produced under strict quality control pathways, ensuring compliance with **UL 94-V0 flammability limits**, **ROHS/REACH directives**, and high tensile strength standards.
