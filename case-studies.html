How we engineered high-volume solutions for some of India's biggest brands.

Electronics / Telecom

Reliance Jio — 12 Million Charger Housings

Challenge: Deliver millions of high-durability flame retardant charger housings with a very tight production schedule and micron-perfect structural assembly limits.

Solution: Fabricated multi-cavity hot runner injection moulds in-house. Deployed a dedicated array of high-volume Haitian injection machines working in 24/7 rotational shifts.

Outcome: Completed production of 12 Million units without delivery delays, meeting all structural drop-testing and flame-retardant compliance standards.

LED & Lighting Sector

Philips & Orient Electric — 30 Million+ Units Annually

Challenge: Produce high-clarity polycarbonate linear diffusers and weather-resistant street light covers matching strict luminosity targets.

Solution: Deployed high-transmittance PC profile extrusion tooling and optimized flow geometries to avoid optical distortion or cooling stress marks.

Outcome: Replaced imported components with locally manufactured products of higher UV durability, scaling to over 30 Million units per year.

Defense & Government

ECIL — 24 EVM Moulds Developed

Challenge: Fabricate highly secure, high-precision structural injection moulds for Indian Electronic Voting Machine (EVM) outer frames, with zero margin for dimension deviations.

Solution: High-precision machining centers (DMG Mori & Makino) used under strict climate-controlled conditions to machine hard-treated tool steel inserts.

Outcome: Supplied 24 verified EVM outer moulds matching internal audit requirements from the Election Commission of India.

Footwear / Consumer

Adidas, Ecco & Bata — 3 Million+ PU Sole Pairs

Challenge: Mass produce high-durability polyurethane shoe soles meeting fashion-grade surface quality and precise Shore hardness specifications for global footwear brands.

Solution: Deployed BASF Elastogran and Desma PU dosing systems to ensure controlled mixing ratios, consistent density, and accurate pour weights within 1% tolerance.

Outcome: Became a preferred local vendor for leading global footwear brands, producing over 3 Million pairs annually with zero quality rejects on consignments.
