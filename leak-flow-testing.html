100% Inline Integrity & Flow Resistance Testing

Fluid transfer systems, IV infusion sets, check valves, respiratory masks, and diagnostic micro-fluidic cassettes demand 100% leak-proof assurance prior to patient delivery. Vidhata Plastics integrates multi-station differential pressure leak decay and mass extraction instruments directly into our ISO Class 7 cleanrooms.

Our automated testing stations use high-resolution transducer sensors capable of measuring micro-leaks down to ≤ 0.1 sccm ($1\text{ }\mu\text{m}$ equivalent orifice). Concurrent differential pressure flow testing verifies lumen opening pressure and detects micro-occlusions, guaranteeing 100% compliant fluidic pathways.
